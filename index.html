Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-11-27

This Privacy Policy explains how the “Blue Eagles – Team Manager” app (“the App”) handles personal data of users and team members.

1. Data Controller

The App is a private tool used only by the Blue Eagles amateur basketball team. For any privacy questions or data deletion requests, please contact:
Email: andrew.negrini@gmail.com

2. Data We Collect

The App may process the following data:

  • Account data: email address, display name, authentication ID (via Supabase).
  • Team roster data: player names, jersey numbers, positions, short bios, optional profile photos.
  • Game data: opponents, dates, locations, notes, scores, convocations, per-player game stats.
  • User-generated content: team photos and album descriptions.
  • Technical identifiers: Supabase user ID and push notification tokens (via OneSignal).

3. Purpose of Processing

All data is used exclusively to provide the App’s functionality, including:

  • Managing the season schedule and game information.
  • Displaying the team roster and player profiles.
  • Recording and reviewing game statistics.
  • Sharing internal team photos and albums.
  • Sending team-related push notifications (practice changes, game reminders, new albums).

Data is not used for advertising, tracking, or analytics.

4. Legal Basis and Scope

The App is not directed to children and access is restricted to invited team members, staff, and relatives. Use of the App is based on the legitimate interest of the team to organize activities and communicate internally.

5. Third-Party Services

The App relies on the following providers acting as processors:

  • Supabase – authentication, database, and file storage (team data and media).
  • OneSignal – delivery of push notifications using device tokens.

These services process data only on behalf of the App and do not use it for their own marketing or profiling, according to their own privacy policies.

6. Storage, Security and Location

Data is stored on Supabase databases and storage buckets and protected by standard technical and organizational measures. All communication with the backend services is encrypted using HTTPS. Access to the App is restricted to authenticated users.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

Data is retained as long as the team uses the App or until deletion is requested. Users or players may ask for correction or removal of their data by contacting the email address provided above. Upon request, the developer will remove or anonymize the relevant records from Supabase and revoke push notification tokens.

8. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 and is not publicly available. Only invited users with valid credentials can access the App. If you believe data about a child has been collected without authorization, please contact us so we can delete it.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top of the page will be modified accordingly. Continued use of the App after any changes means you accept the revised policy.

10. Contact

For any questions, requests, or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact:
Email: andrew.negrini@gmail.com
